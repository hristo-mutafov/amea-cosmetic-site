A LITTLE MORE ABOUT ME

I am Nataliya Nenchova, a beauty professional with over 20 years of experience. For the last 7+ years, I have been devoted to the art of permanent makeup and aesthetic procedures with a paramedical focus. I am a certified specialist in procedures like microblading, eyebrow micropigmentation, eyeliner and lip pigmentation, lash extensions, and medical camouflage. In 2022, I founded my own academy for accredited training in lash extensions and permanent makeup Esthetic Line Academy. Since 2023, I have also been a training coach at the Amiea Academy Bulgaria.

As a woman, I believe that confidence and self-assurance are fundamental to achieving well-being and success. As a professional and expert in the world of aesthetic procedures, I understand that building these qualities requires perseverance, discipline, and attention to detail. I believe in the necessity of having the right team of specialists, whose expertise we can trust when it comes to caring for our appearance. That’s why, as both a woman and a professional, I stand by my principles — beauty, perfectionism, high hygiene standards, and continuous improvement.

My internal drive for constant development led me, in 2022, to create Esthetic Line Academy, where I offer accredited training in permanent makeup and lash extensions. Additionally, since 2023, as a certified medical camouflage trainer, I share my expertise through an exclusive Master Class program at Amiea Academy Bulgaria. Medical camouflage is a unique procedure that corrects imperfections caused by injuries, surgeries, or vitiligo. I am happy to share that within four months, 20 students from all over Bulgaria have been trained by my standards of quality and passion for the procedure. Becoming a trainer has proven to be my calling and inspiration!

Since 2023, I have had the privilege of working in partnership with Amiea Academy Bulgaria. The German brand has 20 years of experience in the PMU industry — offering the highest quality products and being the only permanent makeup company in the world certified by medical standards. I chose to work with them because I resonate with their motto “INNOVATION IS OUR PASSION; PERFECTION IS OUR GOAL.”

The next challenge for me and my academy is tricopigmentation — an aesthetic procedure with a mission that I will tell you much more about very soon…

Join me in the world of flawless beauty, where every imperfection becomes a unique story of transformation!

WHAT I STAND FOR

With 20 years of experience in the beauty industry, 7 of which in active permanent makeup practice, and in the last year as a training coach, I have seen a lot, experimented, tested, created, and trained. I have undergone numerous trainings because, for me, the greatest investment is in quality education. I continue to learn, striving to gain knowledge from the best.
